About OrganicMart

Founded in 2015, OrganicMart connects health-conscious consumers with the finest organic produce from local farms. We believe in sustainable agriculture that benefits both people and the planet.

Our mission is to make organic food accessible while supporting farmers who practice ethical and environmentally-friendly farming methods.

  • 100% Organic Certification
  • Direct from Farm to Your Table
  • Eco-Friendly Packaging
  • Satisfaction Guarantee

Our Journey

OrganicMart was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to connect health-conscious consumers with the finest organic produce from local farms. We believe in sustainable agriculture that benefits both people and the planet.

Today, we serve over 50,000 customers across India with fresh, certified organic products delivered right to their doorstep. Our network includes over 200 local farmers who practice traditional and organic farming methods, ensuring that every product meets our strict quality standards.

What started as a small farmers market stall has now grown into a trusted brand with delivery services in 25 major cities. We're proud to support local farming communities while providing our customers with the healthiest, most delicious organic produce available.

Our team personally visits every farm we partner with, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable farming methods. From seasonal fruits and vegetables to organic grains, pulses, and spices - we bring the best of nature's harvest to your table.

Join us in our journey towards a healthier, more sustainable future. Every purchase you make supports local farmers and helps preserve traditional farming practices for generations to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OrganicMart

Are all products organic certified?

Yes, all products are certified organic by recognized authorities. We work with:

  • NPOP (National Programme for Organic Production)
  • USDA Organic (for imported products)
  • Jaivik Bharat certification

Look for the organic certification logo on each product page.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in two ways:

  1. Via Website: Go to My Account > My Orders > Track Order
  2. Via SMS: You'll receive tracking link on your registered mobile number
  3. Via Email: Check your email for tracking updates

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Sunday: No Deliveries

Is there a minimum order value?

Yes, the minimum order value is ₹99. Orders below ₹99 cannot be placed.

Free delivery is available on orders above ₹499.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods:

Visa Mastercard Amex Google Pay Apple Pay UPI Cash on Delivery

How do I return an item?

To return an eligible item:

  1. Go to My Orders in your account
  2. Click on Return Item next to the product
  3. Select return reason and submit
  4. Schedule pickup or drop-off

Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.

Do you offer subscription plans?

Yes! We offer weekly and monthly subscription plans for regular purchases:

  • Weekly Box: 10% discount + free delivery
  • Monthly Box: 15% discount + free delivery + exclusive items

Visit our Subscription Page to learn more.

How can I verify organic certification?

Each organic product includes:

  • Organic certification logo on product page
  • Certification number and FSSAI license details
  • QR code on packaging for verification

You can scan the QR code to view the complete certification details.
